# Lemon Squeezy (Digital Products)

> Sell digital products globally — Lemon Squeezy handles VAT, tax, and compliance

## Why This Exists

If you sell digital products globally, you legally must collect VAT in each country. Lemon Squeezy makes you a Merchant of Record — they collect and remit tax for you. The alternative is registering in 80 countries.
